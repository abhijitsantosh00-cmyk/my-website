Kalyanpur Ganesh puja

Village Ganesh Puja is a joyful and traditional festival celebrated with great devotion and unity. The villagers come together to bring a Ganesh idol, usually made of clay, and place it in a common area like a temple or open ground. Everyone contributes money, decorations, and time. The puja includes prayers, bhajans, drum beats, and cultural programs such as dance and songs. Children and elders participate with happiness and respect. Modaks and other sweets are prepared and shared among all. On the last day, the idol is taken for visarjan in a river or pond with prayers. Village Ganesh Puja strengthens friendship, cooperation, and cultural values.
